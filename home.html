Christmas Help 2014


Mødrehjælpen awards again this year Christmas help for the financially strapper families,so they can get the opportunity to celebrate Christmas.

Christmas Help

Mødrehjælpen is making a Christmas Campaign to help the families have a good July by collecting a sum of money.It can be difficult for the families that cannot afford to keep Christmas special for the Children. The Christmas month is very important in children's everyday life in December, and it can be hard to be the only such class or kindergarten who do not get gifts or Christmas Eve. A family that makes Christmas help, typically 2000-3000 dollars per. months back when the fixed costs are paid. The amount can cover a modest household budget, but it is not enough for holidays, birthdays, recreation and Christmas.

How do we Help?

Mothers Help give 400 dollars per. child for Christmas, and in addition get the family an amount for food of 300 kr. + 100 kr. for each additional child. This means that a family with two children is (400 + 400 + 300 + 100) 1,200 kr. The families do not pay tax on the amount. The other half of the money that goes to the long-term effort to contribute to that we can give help and advice to families, so in the long term are fewer families who can not afford to celebrate July Will there be more money than there are eligible applicants for, the money will also go to long-term help to support families to resolve the underlying problems.

Who do we Help?

There are clear criteria for when an applicant is eligible for Christmas help. Applicants must have children under the age of 18. In addition, they have low disposable income and having been out for a special event this year to make daily life and the economy difficult to grasp. Both mothers and fathers can apply, but we have limited funds for Christmas help and can only give to those families most in need. All may turn to Mødrehjælpen for advice.
